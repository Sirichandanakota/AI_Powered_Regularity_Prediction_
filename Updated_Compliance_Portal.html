Help & Documentation

Welcome to the Help & Documentation section of the Enterprise AI Compliance Portal. Here you can find resources to assist you in navigating and utilizing the portal effectively.

1. Portal Overview

The Enterprise AI Compliance Portal provides real-time regulatory prediction, automated compliance actions, and comprehensive analytics to ensure proactive adherence to legal frameworks in India's e-commerce sector. It's built to empower teams with actionable insights and reduce compliance risks.

2. Key Features

  • **Dashboard Overview:** Get a quick glance at system health, compliance rates, and last updates. This section provides a high-level summary of the portal's operational status.
  • **Critical Alerts & Notifications:** Receive immediate alerts on potential compliance risks. These alerts are prioritized by severity and include AI-driven recommendations for action.
  • **Predictive Analytics:** Analyze trends in compliance, risk scores, and regulatory changes. Our AI models forecast future regulatory shifts to help you stay ahead.
  • **Regulatory Risk & Impact Visuals:** Visualize evolving risks and departmental exposure through interactive charts and heatmaps, offering a clear picture of potential vulnerabilities.
  • **Control Panel:** Manage AI configurations, initiate monitoring, and generate reports. This central hub allows fine-tuning of the AI engine and access to critical reporting tools.
  • **Automated Actions Log:** Review a detailed history of all automated compliance interventions. This immutable log ensures transparency and auditability of all AI-driven actions.
  • **Legal Review & Human Oversight Queue:** A dedicated queue for alerts requiring human legal review and validation, ensuring a balanced approach between AI automation and expert human judgment.
  • **AI Engine Modules Status:** Monitor the operational status of individual AI components, providing transparency into the health and confidence levels of each module.

3. Troubleshooting Tips

  • **Data Not Refreshing:** If data appears stale, click the "Refresh Data" button in the Quick Actions sidebar on the left. This will trigger a manual data synchronization.
  • **Chart Errors:** If charts are not displaying correctly, ensure your browser is up-to-date. Try clearing your browser cache. If issues persist, please contact support with a screenshot.
  • **Login Issues:** Double-check your Employee ID and ensure the password is 'securepass'. If you've forgotten your password, contact your IT administrator for a reset.
  • **Search Not Working:** Try refining your search term to be more specific. The search functionality provides recommendations as you type; utilize these for better results.
  • **Performance Slowdown:** If the portal feels slow, try closing other heavy browser tabs. If the issue continues, it might be a temporary system load; please report it to support.
